Overview

The 1997 Toyota RAV4 was part of the firt generation of this well known modern RAV4. It was the first crossover SUVs that appealed to most drivers since it had a big roomy interior, and wasa fuel efficient.

Features

The 1997 Toyota RAV4 came with some exciting features that make it one of the best SUV ever created, they indlude:

  • Removable real spare placed on the back of the car creating more cargo space
  • It was designed with 2 door and 4 doors options
  • Standard footwheel drive with many of them being all wheel drive
  • The design was really sporty with luxury feel uniqueness.

Specifications

The techical specifications of the 1997 RAV4 included:

  • Egine: 2.0L
  • Transmission: 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic
  • Drivetrain: FWD or AWD
  • Seating: UP to 5 passeengers
  • Horsepower: 120hp
  • Fuel consumption: 29 MPG highway
